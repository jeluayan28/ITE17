How VibeCheck Works

Our comprehensive system provides end-to-end monitoring and protection for your mining operations.

1

Installation

Our team installs a network of high-sensitivity acoustic sensors throughout your mine, strategically placed to capture comprehensive data.

2

Data Collection

Sensors continuously collect acoustic data, capturing subtle vibrations, sounds, and structural movements that human ears can't detect.

3

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI algorithms process the acoustic data in real-time, identifying patterns that indicate potential structural weaknesses or failures.

4

Alert & Response

When potential hazards are detected, the system immediately alerts mine operators through multiple channels, enabling rapid response.
